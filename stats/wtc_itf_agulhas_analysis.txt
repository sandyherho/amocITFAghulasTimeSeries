PHYSICAL INTERPRETATION:
================================================
The wavelet coherence analysis reveals multi-scale interactions between
Indonesian Throughflow (ITF) components and Agulhas system variability.

KEY FINDINGS:
1. ITF Salinity - Agulhas Box (panel a):
   - Indicates freshwater flux teleconnections
   - High coherence suggests Indo-Pacific freshwater export impacts
     Indian Ocean salinity structure

2. ITF Temperature - Agulhas Box (panel b):
   - Reveals thermal connections between ocean basins
   - Coherent variability indicates heat transport pathways
   - Important for regional climate modulation

3. ITF Geostrophic - Agulhas Jet (panel c):
   - Direct dynamical connection between volume transports
   - Suggests common forcing mechanisms (e.g., wind stress)

4. ITF Geostrophic - Agulhas Box (panel d):
   - Broader regional impact of ITF variability
   - Indicates basin-scale circulation adjustments

PHASE RELATIONSHIPS:
- In-phase behavior: Simultaneous response to common forcing
- Phase lags: Indicate propagation time of signals
- Anti-phase: Possible compensating mechanisms

DOMINANT TIMESCALES:
- Sub-annual: Seasonal monsoon forcing
- 1-2 years: ENSO teleconnections
- 3-7 years: Indian Ocean Dipole and ENSO interactions
- Decadal: Pacific Decadal Oscillation influence
